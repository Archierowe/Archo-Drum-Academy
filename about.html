About - Meet the founder

A professional drummer with a wealth of playing experience, Matt founded Arco Drum Academy in 2019. Taught to play his very first beat at 12 years old by his Uncle who grew up playing in Parisian Jazz bars, Matt was hooked. From the age of 17 Matt went pro and has played in the bands Coalesce, Deltaville and The Thinking Men. He has enjoyed commercial success, playing alongside artists and bands such as Coldplay, Elbow and Massive Attack and was signed to Warner Music for 7 years.

Matt has recorded 7 studio albums and has an impressive CV which includes sessions at the world-renowned Ronnie Scotts and playing for Grammy-winning artists at the MOBO awards and the BRITS. He now brings his passion and experience of playing to teaching full-time, starting Arco Drums in 2019.

The studios, based in Norwich, offer state of the art equipment and a staff of exceptional tutors, all of whom, Matt has gathered around him through his passion and dedication to making learning drums fun, vibrant and accessible. Playing drums is exciting, and can bring some of the most amazing experiences to your life! Come, learn, be the best you can be! Follow your dream from studio to stage.
